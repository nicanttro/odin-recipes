Marinated Pork Roast

Description

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon lemon pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seed
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon celery salt
  • 1 clover garlic, minced
  • 1 (4 pound) pork roast
  • cooking spray

How To Make

  1. Combine Worcestershire sauce, honey, vinegar, lemon pepper, mustard seed, mustard powder, celery salt, and garlic in a large resealable plastic bag. Seal and mix Ingredients.
  2. Place pork roast in the plastic bag, press air out of bag, and seal. Marinate for 2 hours in the refrigerator, turning the roast occasionally to help coat while marinating.
  3. Prepare grill for indirect heat.
  4. Lightly oil grill grate. Place roast on the grill and discard marinade. Cover and cook until internal temperatures is 145 degrees F (63 degrees C), 1 1/2 to 2 hours.
