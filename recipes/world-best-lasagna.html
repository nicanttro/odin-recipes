World Best Lasagna

Description

This recipe was submitted by John Chandler more than 20 years ago on Allrecipes. It's one of the most popular recipes on Allrecipes, racking in more than 7 million views per year.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 lb lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans of tomato sauce
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 lb mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

How to Make

  1. Overview
    1. Make the meat sauce.
    2. Cook the noodles.
    3. Make the ricotta mixture.
    4. Layer the lasagna according to recipe instructions.
    5. Cover with foil and bake.
    6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
  2. Making Meat Sauce
    1. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a Dutch oven over medium heat until well browned.
    2. Stir in tomato ingredients and water. Season with sugar, 2 tablespoons parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmer, covered, for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  3. Cooking Lasagna Noodles
    • Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water.
  4. Making Ricotta Mixture
    • In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining 2 tablespoons parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  5. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  6. Layering Lasagna
    1. 1 1/2 cup meat sauce, spread
    2. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise, slight overlap
    3. Spread 1/2 ricotta mixture
    4. 1/3 mozzarella slices
    5. 1 1/2 cup Meat sauce, spread
    6. 1/4 Parmesan cheese, sprinkle
    7. Repeat steps 1-6, then top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan
  7. Baking
    • Cover with foil: either spay foil with cooking spray or make sure foil doesn't touch cheese
    • Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes
    • Remove foil and bake for 25 minutes
    • Let lasagna rest for 15 minutes
